Apple Strudel

Description

Apple strudel is a traditional Viennese strudel, a popular pastry in Austria, Bavaria, the Czech Republic, Northern Italy, Slovenia, Croatia, and other countries in Europe that once belonged to the Austro-Hungarian Empire (1867â€“1918).

Ingredients

  • Puff pastry
  • 300g Apricot jam
  • 3 Apples
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Crushed cookies
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Peel and cut the apples in thin slices.
  2. Roll out the puff pastry with a rolling pin.
  3. Spread the jam over the puff pastry with a spoon.
  4. Arrange the apple slices over the jam.
  5. Sprinkle with cinnamon and cookies.
  6. Cut tiny pieces of ubtter and arrange them over the apple slices.
  7. Roll the puff pastry edges over, overlapping them.
  8. Bake for around 40 minutes at 180 degrees C (360 degrees F).
